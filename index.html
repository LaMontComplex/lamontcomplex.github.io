The Groups

Notices group - All residents should consider joining the Notices group - this is where the Trustees send out important messages. Chat is limited to Administrators to minimize disturbance.

Alerts - This group is for security and emergency related matters only. Please do not use this group for anything else.

Community - This group is for general chit-chat, asking questions and is open for all residents.

The Rules

  1. Respect the purpose of each group. If unsure use a personal message.
  2. Communicate between 06:00 and 21:00 only unless an emergency/security issue.
  3. Do not send content, information or “news” that has not been verified as true.
  4. No messages with bad/foul language or unsavoury pictures will be tolerated.
  5. No messages with political, denominational, religious or racist undertones will be tolerated.
  6. No advertising.
  7. Use emoticons sparingly and only reply to messages if adding value or seeking more information.
  8. Don’t respond with Thanks and Noted - reading the message is acknowledgment.
  9. If the group(s) are too busy, consider muting the the notifications.
  10. Contact the administrator if unsure or you have questions.

Advertising

The trustees have decided to allow advertising for a month on the Community WhatsApp group. The following limitations apply:

  1. Advertising may commence from 6 May 2020 and the last day will be 6 June 2020.
  2. Ensure advert contains a photo, telephone number and the unit it relates to.
  3. Keep business transactions out of Community group, rather chat one-on-one privately.
  4. Abide by the lockdown laws, maintain 1.5m social distance and sanitize products and money after exchange.
  5. If advertising on behalf of sellers outside the complex, it is the responsibility of the unit placing the advert to ensure delivery of product as outsiders accessing the complex must be limited.
  6. The trustees and body corporate take no responsibility for any loss or damage that may occur as a result of this.
